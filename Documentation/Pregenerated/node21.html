The <gsmarkup> tag

The document begins with a <gsmarkup> tag, and ends with a </gsmarkup>. So, here is a skeleton gsmarkup file: 
<?xml version="1.0"?>
<!DOCTYPE gsmarkup>
<gsmarkup>
  <!-- the document goes here -->
</gsmarkup>
(this <gsmarkup> tag is the equivalent of the <html> tag in HTML.
